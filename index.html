T1548 - Abuse Elevation Control Mechanism

MITRE ATT&CK Technique Details

High Severity

Description

Adversaries may circumvent mechanisms designed to control elevated privileges to gain higher-level permissions. This technique involves exploiting weaknesses in permission and privilege management systems, often targeting setuid binaries, scheduled tasks, or exploiting vulnerabilities in privilege escalation prevention mechanisms.

Detection Strategy

  • Monitor for unexpected processes running with elevated privileges
  • Track modifications to sudoers file or User Account Control settings
  • Analyze system logs for privilege escalation attempts
